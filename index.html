Who am I

Shige Fukushima is an engineer with deep technical, business and evangelism experience in digital media delivery, network security, server virtualization and cloud infrastructure, and system operation/management/automation infrastructure. Shige has a proven track record finding strategically sensible, cost-effective customer solutions using the right combination of international and industrial technologies, while providing insights and feedbacks back to engineering organizations on changing feature requirements. That was done not only by the consulting way based on the customers/partners’ requirements but also by the strategical way to the market with building a business/technical consensus in the ecosystem. Shige has been a good technical communicating bridge between the engineering team and the customers/partners for the technology deployment into the market.
Specialties: Content protection, server infrastructure virtualization/operation/management, technology evangelist, partnership development, program management, strategic planning and marketing
Industry: Software and hardware, IT consulting/services, consumer digital media, enterprise system deployment/management.
